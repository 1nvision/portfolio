Magazine Cover
---
Created a mock magazine cover for a fake magazine company with event that had been happening in the Esports scene at that time.  I grabbed a picture off the internet and then adjusted the lighting and values of the image in Lightroom. I then put the image into photoshop and added  the text that were specific events happening at that time. Along with a name for the Magazine that I made up based on what the topics were related to.

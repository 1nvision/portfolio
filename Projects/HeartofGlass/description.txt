Heart Of Glass
---
Created this piece when I was going through a rough patch and it felt like my heart had been shattered by a bullet. So I put that feeling into an image. Started with an outline of a heart along with a sketch of a bullet lodged in the center but changed it to be behind the heart like it was shot completely through. Started by filling the heart with the darker colors first like dark turquoise and sketching where the glass would be shattered and where the hole would be. After filling it in with the main 5 colors I started adding lighter shades and highlights to give the impression of the heart being glass. I then added a border around the edges of the image to give the impression of it being a picture of some sort. Then at the end I added some extra details like smoke and red on the floor as “splatter”.

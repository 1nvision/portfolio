My name is Collin Savall and I am an artist who has been shaped by years of working across many different mediums and a deep passion for creating art that lives inside games and interactive experiences. Growing up I was always making something, drawing on homework, shaping clay, or carving wood. The 8 years of hands-on work taught me how to think through a creative problem and follow it all the way through. When I ended up transitioning into digital art in high school, that same instinct carried over into illustration, photo editing, video editing, 3D modeling, and animation (as well as a little bit of sound design). Working across so many mediums has made me a more adaptable and well-rounded artist, and I take pride in being able to pick up new tools and techniques driven by nothing more than my own curiosity. 

Games and interactive media are where this curiosity found its home. What intrigues me about this is that the art has a purpose beyond looking good, a character or environment has to function inside a larger experience and hold up when someone is actively engaging with it. Studying Art, Games, and Playable Media at UC Santa Cruz has deepened that passion and given me the skills to create work built for that kind of use. My background across traditional and digital mediums gives me a strong foundation, and my love for games gives me direction. Together they push me to create work that is both carefully crafted and built to be experienced.
